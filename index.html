💻 Real-World Projects for CCNA, CCNP & CCIE Courses

We’re taking our training to the next level! From now on, all major networking courses — CCNA, CCNP, and CCIE — will include real-world project work at regular intervals.

  • Access to real networking equipment
  • Hands-on experience configuring and troubleshooting live networks
  • Upon passing lab tests, you’ll earn:
    • Participation Certificate for the course
    • Project Completion Certificate showcasing your practical skills

This means you'll walk away with resume-worthy, real-world experience 👨‍💻👩‍💻
